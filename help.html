Welcome to the GyroTime Help Page

If you need assistance navigating our website or placing an order, you're in the right place. Below are common questions and helpful tips.

How to Navigate the Website

  • Home Button: Click the GyroTime logo at the top to return to the homepage.
  • Menu: Use the navigation links at the top to access pages like About Us, Menu, Help, and Accessibility.
  • Search: Use the search bar at the top right to find specific dishes or info.

How to Place an Order

  • Select your dishes from the menu and click to add to your cart.
  • Click the cart icon at the top right to review your order.
  • Follow checkout prompts to enter delivery and payment info.
  • You will get an email confirmation once your order is placed.

Accessibility Features

  • Use browser zoom to enlarge text for better readability.
  • The site supports keyboard navigation and screen readers.
  • Visit our Accessibility page for more options.

Contact Support

  • Live Chat: Available during business hours via the chat icon.

Need More Help?
