About Us

GyroTime is a family-owned restaurant dedicated to serving authentic Greek cuisine. We use homemade recipes filled with rich flavors and fresh ingredients. Our goal is to provide a welcoming environment where everyone can enjoy delicious food, whether you prefer meat, vegetarian, or vegan options.

We take pride in our secret spice recipes that make our gyros and dishes special. Whether you're dining in or ordering online, GyroTime aims to give you a taste of Greece with every bite.

GyroTime is located in Wakefield, serving some of the best gyros in the country.

Thank you for visiting GyroTime! We look forward to serving you and sharing our love for Greek food.
