Accessibility at GyroTime

At GyroTime, we are committed to making our website accessible to everyone, including individuals with disabilities. Our goal is to provide an inclusive online experience that allows all users to navigate, understand, and enjoy our content easily.

Accessibility Features

1. Text Size and Font Options

  • Adjustable text size: Users can increase or decrease font size for better readability.
  • Clear, readable fonts: We use simple, sans-serif fonts to enhance readability.

2. Color Contrast and Visual Design

  • High contrast mode: Our website offers a high contrast option to improve visibility for users with visual impairments.
  • Color schemes: We use color combinations that meet WCAG (Web Content Accessibility Guidelines) standards for contrast.

3. Keyboard Navigation

  • Users can navigate all pages and features using keyboard shortcuts, ensuring accessibility for those who cannot use a mouse.

4. Screen Reader Compatibility

  • Our website is designed to be compatible with screen readers, with descriptive alt text for images and ARIA labels for navigation elements.

5. Alternative Content

  • Text-based equivalents are provided for all non-text content, including images and icons.
  • Transcripts and captions are available for multimedia content where applicable.

6. Accessibility Toolbar

An accessibility toolbar is available on every page, allowing users to:

  • Adjust font size
  • Switch to high contrast mode
  • Enable screen reader support
